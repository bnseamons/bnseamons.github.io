JARL(Just Another Robotic Limb)

JARL is an prosthetic arm I have been developing for a quadriplegic patient. The current model has 5 degrees of freedom and mounts to side of a wheel chair and allows users to perform tasks such as pushing elevator buttons and drinking from a cup. The user operates from an Android app that sends commands to our arm which is powered by an Arduino.
