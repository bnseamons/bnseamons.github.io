I am currently the team lead for JARL, a project where my team aims to build a prosthetic arm for a quadriplegic patient. The assembly of our 5 axis arm mounts to side of a wheel chair and allows users to perform tasks such as pushing elevator buttons and drinking from a cup. The user operates the arm via controls on the app I designed for Android. This allows the user's phone to send commands to our arm which is powered by an Arduino.
